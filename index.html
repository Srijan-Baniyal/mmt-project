Multimedia Elements

Text

Text is the most common form of multimedia. It is used to convey information, provide instructions, and tell stories.

Images

Images are used to illustrate concepts, provide visual interest, and enhance the user experience. They can be photographs, illustrations, or graphics.

Audio

Audio includes music, sound effects, and spoken words. It is used to add depth and emotion to multimedia content.

Video

Video combines moving images and sound to tell stories, provide instructions, and entertain. It is a powerful medium for communication.

Animations

Animations are sequences of images or drawings that create the illusion of movement. They are used to illustrate concepts, tell stories, and entertain.
